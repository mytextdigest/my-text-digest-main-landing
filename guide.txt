## 1. Overall Design Philosophy 🌌

**My Text Digest** should feel like:

* A **next-generation AI research tool**
* **Private, secure, intelligent**
* Built for **professionals, researchers, lawyers, developers, students**
* Comparable to **Apple × OpenAI × Linear-level polish**

**Core Emotions**

* Trust 🔐
* Intelligence 🧠
* Power ⚡
* Calm & Focus 🎯

**Design Keywords**

> Dark • Minimal • Futuristic • Immersive • Premium • Scientific

## 2. Color System & Theme 🎨

### Primary Theme (Default)

* **Deep Space Black** `#05060A`
* **Midnight Blue** `#0B1020`
* **Neural Cyan** `#00E5FF`
* **Electric Violet** `#7C7CFF`
* **Soft White Text** `#EDEDED`

* Gradient glows (cyan → violet)
* Subtle animated light rays
* Hover neon outlines

🌓 Light mode exists but **Dark Mode is the hero**
